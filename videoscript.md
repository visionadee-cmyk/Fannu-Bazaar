## Tips for InVideo:

1. **Aspect Ratio:** Always use 9:16 (vertical) for TikTok
2. **Duration:** Keep videos 15-60 seconds for best engagement
3. **Music:** Use trending, upbeat, or tropical music
4. **Text:** Keep text large, readable, and minimal
5. **Colors:** Use app's brand colors (green/blue theme)
6. **CTA:** Always include clear call-to-action at the end
7. **Captions:** Enable auto-captions for accessibility
8. **Hashtags:** Add #FannuBazaar #Maldives #ServiceApp #MaldivesWorkers
