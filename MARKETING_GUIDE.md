# Fannu Bazaar Marketing Guide

This guide explains how to open the Fannu Bazaar Facebook page and how to create and add posters to promote the app.

## Opening the Fannu Bazaar Facebook Page

1. **Visit Facebook**
   - Go to [facebook.com](https://facebook.com)
   - Log in to your Facebook account

2. **Search for Fannu Bazaar**
   - In the Facebook search bar, type "Fannu Bazaar"
   - Look for the official Fannu Bazaar page
   - Click on the page to open it

3. **Alternative: Direct Link**
   - If you have the direct URL, paste it in your browser
   - Make sure you have admin access to manage the page

## Creating and Adding Posters for Promotion

### Step 1: Download the QR Code

1. **Open the QR Code HTML File**
   - Navigate to: `public/qr-code.html` in your project folder
   - Right-click the file and open it in your browser
   - Or run the app locally and visit: `http://localhost:5173/qr-code.html`

2. **Download the QR Code Image**
   - Click the blue "Save QR Code" button
   - The QR code will be downloaded as `fannu-bazaar-qr-code.png`
   - Alternatively, right-click the QR code image and select "Save image as..."

### Step 2: Create Your Poster

#### Option A: Using Canva (Recommended)

1. **Go to Canva**
   - Visit [canva.com](https://canva.com)
   - Sign up or log in to your account

2. **Choose a Template**
   - Search for "Poster" templates
   - Select a template that fits your brand (use green colors to match Fannu Bazaar theme)
   - Or start with a blank custom size (A4 or 18x24 inches recommended)

3. **Add Your Content**
   - **Title**: "Fannu Bazaar" or "Download Fannu Bazaar"
   - **Subtitle**: "Find skilled workers or offer your services"
   - **Description**: Add a brief description of the app in English and/or Dhivehi
   - **QR Code**: Upload the downloaded `fannu-bazaar-qr-code.png` and place it prominently
   - **Call to Action**: "Scan to download" or "Scan now to get started"
   - **Contact Info**: Add your Facebook page URL, phone number, or email

4. **Design Tips**
   - Use the Fannu Bazaar brand colors:
     - Primary green: `#10B981`
     - Light green: `#ECFDF5`
     - Dark text: `#111827`
   - Keep the design clean and easy to read
   - Make the QR code large enough to scan easily (at least 2x2 inches)
   - Ensure high contrast for accessibility

5. **Download the Poster**
   - Click "Download" in Canva
   - Choose PNG or PDF format (high quality)
   - Select "Standard quality" or "High quality"

#### Option B: Using Other Design Tools

- **Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator**: Create professional designs
- **Microsoft PowerPoint/Word**: Simple designs
- **Figma**: Free design tool with templates
- **Google Slides**: Basic poster creation

### Step 3: Add Poster to Facebook Page

1. **Go to Your Fannu Bazaar Facebook Page**
   - Log in to Facebook
   - Navigate to the Fannu Bazaar page
   - Make sure you have admin or editor permissions

2. **Create a Post**
   - Click "Create post" or "What's on your mind?"
   - Click "Photo/Video" to upload your poster
   - Select the poster file you created

3. **Write Your Post Caption**

   **English:**
   ```
   🚀 Introducing Fannu Bazaar - Your One-Stop Service Platform!

   Find skilled workers for any job or offer your services to the community. 
   From home repairs to professional services, we've got you covered.

   📱 Scan the QR code to download the app now!
   👉 Or visit: https://fannu-bazaar.vercel.app

   #FannuBazaar #Maldives #Services #Workers #JobMarket
   ```

   **Dhivehi:**
   ```
   🚀 ފަނު ބަޒާރ - ކޮމްޕިއުޓަރައިޒް ސަރވިސް ޕްލެޓްފޯމް!

   ކޮމްޕިއުޓަރައިޒް ސަރވިސް ހޯދުމަށް ނުވަތަ ތިޔަ ހުންނެވި ސަރވިސް ފޮނުވައިދިނުމަށް.
   އަހަރެއްގެ މައްޗަށް ސަރވިސް ހޯދަން، ފަނު ބަޒާރ އަކީ ތިޔަގެ އެންމެ ރަނގަޅު އިންތިހެންތިއެވެ.

   📱 ކޯޑް ސްކެން ކޮށް އެޕް ޑައުންލޯޑް ކުރޭ!
   👉 ނުވަތަ: https://fannu-bazaar.vercel.app

   #ފަނުބަޒާރ #މާލެދިވެހިރާއްޖެ #ސަރވިސް
   ```

4. **Add Location (Optional)**
   - Click "Check in" to add your location
   - This helps people find your business locally

5. **Post the Content**
   - Click "Post" to publish
   - Your poster will now be visible on your Facebook page

### Step 4: Boost Your Post (Optional)

1. **Boost the Post**
   - After posting, click "Boost post"
   - Set your target audience (location, age, interests)
   - Set your budget (minimum amount varies by country)
   - Choose duration for the boost
   - Click "Boost" to run the ad

2. **Target Audience Suggestions**
   - **Location**: Maldives (specific atolls if targeting locally)
   - **Age**: 18-65 (adults who need services)
   - **Interests**: Home improvement, business, services, employment
   - **Language**: English and Dhivehi

## Additional Promotion Ideas

### Physical Posters

1. **Print Your Posters**
   - Use a local printing service
   - Recommended sizes: A4, A3, or larger (18x24 inches)
   - Use high-quality paper for durability

2. **Display Locations**
   - Community centers
   - Local businesses (with permission)
   - Public bulletin boards
   - Ferry terminals
   - Schools and universities
   - Government offices

### Social Media

1. **Share on Other Platforms**
   - Instagram: Post the poster in your feed and stories
   - Twitter/X: Share with relevant hashtags
   - WhatsApp: Share in community groups
   - Telegram: Share in local channels

2. **Create Video Content**
   - Short video explaining how to use the app
   - Screen recording of app features
   - Testimonials from users

### Community Engagement

1. **Host Information Sessions**
   - Organize community meetings
   - Demonstrate the app live
   - Answer questions from potential users

2. **Partner with Local Businesses**
   - Ask businesses to display posters
   - Offer referral incentives
   - Create partnership programs

## Tracking Your Promotion

### Monitor Facebook Insights

1. **Check Post Performance**
   - Go to your Facebook page
   - Click "Insights" in the left menu
   - View reach, engagement, and clicks
   - Track which posts perform best

2. **QR Code Scans**
   - If using a paid QR code service, track scan statistics
   - Monitor app downloads after poster distribution

## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Post regularly (at least 2-3 times per week)
   - Keep your branding consistent
   - Use the same colors and logo

2. **Engage with Your Audience**
   - Respond to comments and messages
   - Ask for feedback
   - Share user testimonials

3. **Use High-Quality Images**
   - Always use high-resolution images
   - Ensure text is readable
   - Test QR codes before printing

4. **Localize Your Content**
   - Create content in both English and Dhivehi
   - Use local references and cultural context
   - Address local needs and pain points

5. **Offer Incentives**
   - Early bird discounts
   - Referral bonuses
   - Free trial periods
